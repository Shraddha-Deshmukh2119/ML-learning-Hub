What is Linear Regression?

Linear regression is a basic and commonly used type of predictive analysis. It is one of the simplest algorithms in machine learning and serves as a good starting point for understanding more complex methods.

The overall idea of regression is to examine two things:

  1. Does a set of predictor variables do a good job in predicting an outcome (dependent) variable?
  2. Which variables in particular are significant predictors of the outcome variable, and in what way do they impact the outcome variable?

Mathematically, linear regression can be represented as:

y = β₀ + β₁x₁ + β₂x₂ + ... + βₙxₙ + ε

Where:

  • y is the dependent variable
  • x₁, x₂, ..., xₙ are the independent variables
  • β₀, β₁, β₂, ..., βₙ are the coefficients that the algorithm learns
  • ε is the error term

Applications:

  • Sales forecasting
  • Risk assessment
  • Real estate price prediction
  • Trend analysis
